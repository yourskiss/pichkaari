Motion Graphics & Video

Motion graphics and videos are now the most powerful marketing tools on the web. Brands can tell a story by borrowing aspects of photography, illustration and typography to create the kind of visually rich compositions that keep viewers engaged.

Motion graphics are a creative way of delivering complex ideas in a simple way that anyone—from children to the elderly—can engage with and understand. Put simply, they’re highly accessible, and consequently a potent marketing tool.

The Spot

The most classic kind of video you can produce is a 15 or 30 second commercial, commonly referred to as a “spot.” The content of a spot can widely vary depending on your brand and the story you want to tell, but the purpose of producing one is simply that it can be distributed virtually everywhere.

The Explainer Video

If your business offers a service or good that isn’t immediately understood, an explainer video on your homepage or a landing page may be just what you need. These videos typically feature animation with a VoiceOver that explains what your business offers to the audience.

Company Culture Videos

Sometimes you need to market your brand not to new customers, but to potential new employees, which can be essential for growing your business. One of the best ways to do this is to capture your most passionate employees on camera.

Behind The Scenes

If you have an interesting process behind the development, manufacturing, or distribution of your goods or services, show it! Your audience will love to see the craftsmanship that goes into what you make.
